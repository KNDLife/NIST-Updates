# NIST SP 800-172 Draft – Enhanced Requirements for Protecting Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)

**Released:** September 29, 2025 (Draft)  
**Purpose:** Strengthens data protection for high-value CUI assets.

## Key Changes
- Adds resilience measures against advanced persistent threats (APTs).
- Introduces continuous monitoring and enhanced encryption standards.
- Improves audit and accountability for federal contractors and partners.

**Plain English:**  
If you handle CUI for the U.S. government, expect stricter rules to keep it safe—especially around monitoring, encryption, and data handling procedures.
